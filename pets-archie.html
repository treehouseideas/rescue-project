Archie

Corgi - Calico - Young - Male
Adoptable Small Spayed/Neutered Up-to-date with routine shots House trained

He is 2 year old Corgi mix, 20 lbs, great with dogs and people. Archie is sweet, playful, and very much loves to be held like a baby. Although he is great with children he will do better with older kids as he still plays like a puppy. Archie doesn't bark at all, enjoys going for walks, and loves to follow you everywhere. We would love him in a household with another friendly companion.

Archie sadly was thrown out of a moving car by horrible people and he is lucky to be alive. He has received full medical including neuter, vaccination, microchip, dental cleaning, heart worm test, fecal test, and de-worming.
